Your Core Construction!

Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj, Mirabai, APJ Abul Kalam, Guru Gobind Singh all had one thing in common. As they were growing up, they were told stories about respect for women, valor, courage, love, devotion, etc. It made them do things that made them larger than life

We typically know them as our value system. When we conduct ourselves, in alignment with our value system, we are at ease, thus we can touch our optimal performance levels. On the contrary, when we have to operate in environments that conflict with our value system, we end up feeling suffocated, our energy drains off, and in the worst case, we may experience depression, etc

Thus it is critical to identify our core values and use them to make critical decisions in our life.

Let us go through an activity to identify our core values.

This will allow us to make critical choices, keeping into consideration our core values. Thus we can expect to experience greater joy, success, and happiness

This helps us in taking actions that are aligned with who we are. Thus we end up feeling better, as there are fewer conflicts
